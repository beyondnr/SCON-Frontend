# **App Name**: SCON (Shift Control)

## Core Features:

- **Onboarding Wizard**: Guides new business owners through a 3-step onboarding process (account, store, employees with shift presets - morning/afternoon/custom).
- **Monthly Schedule Editor**: Manager-driven monthly schedule creation with weekly tab view (hourly view as default) and week pattern copy functionality.
- **Shift Time Validation**: Warning display when assigning employees outside their default shift hours (e.g., "김민준님의 기본 근무 시간은 10:00~18:00입니다.").
- **Schedule Email Notification**: Send completed monthly schedules to employees via email with full calendar preview before sending.
- **Payroll Preview**: Preview payroll calculations based on created schedules, with export options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A0D2EB) to convey trust and stability, aligning with the business management aspect.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F7FA) to provide a clean and uncluttered workspace.
- Accent color: Muted green (#86C8BC) to highlight important actions and success states.
- Warning color: Orange/Yellow for out-of-shift-time assignment warnings.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for headlines.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif font for the main text.
- Simple and clear icons to represent different actions and data points within the application.
- Mobile-first design with a focus on large, easily tappable buttons and clear visual hierarchy.

## MVP Scope:

### Included:
- Monthly schedule creation with weekly tab navigation
- Employee shift preset management (morning 10:00~18:00 / afternoon 13:00~21:00 / custom)
- Week 1 pattern copy to other weeks
- Out-of-shift-time warning display (save allowed)
- Monthly calendar preview before email sending
- Email notification to scheduled employees

### Excluded (Future):
- Employee feedback collection
- Schedule confirmation/approval workflow
- AI-powered auto-scheduling
- Real-time availability submission by employees
